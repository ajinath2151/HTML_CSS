OUR STORY

Dr. Jehangir Gowani, MD. is the CEO and founder of Gargle Water, Inc.

Gargle Water products are physician-formulated by Dr. Jehangir Gowani. His credentials are vast, as he is Board Certified by the American Board of Internal Medicine as well as the American Board of Pediatrics. He is also an esteemed member of the American College of Physicians and American Academy of Pediatrics.

Dr. Gowani served the needs of urgent care patients for over 13 years and consistently found a need to soothe the throat of those suffering from a variety of ailments. From this catalyst, Gargle Water, Inc. was born. Simple and effective, our products are designed as a proactive daily rinse for oral health.

The innate need to offer every family member sustained health, achieved from the comfort of their own home, lead to the development of Gargle Salt Water ™ and Pedia Gargle ™ for children. Salt Water Gargle is a salt-based gargle for fast relief. Our proprietary formula quickly soothes your throat with no alcohol, drugs, or dyes. We invite you to enjoy our gargles in good health.
